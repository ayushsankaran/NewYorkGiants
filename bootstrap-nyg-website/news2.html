Giants agree to terms with C Brett Heggie, DE Raymond Johnson III, OL Jake Burton Giants.com - May 2, 2021

The Giants are continuing to add to their 2021 NFL Draft weekend haul by agreeing to terms with three undrafted free agents, according to media reports. The signings are pending the completion of physicals.

Two offensive linemen have been added - Florida center Brett Heggie and Baylor's Jake Burton.

On the defensive side, the Giants will add Raymond Johnson III, defensive end from Georgia Southern.

These three rookies will join the Giants' 2021 draft class - wide receiver Kadarius Toney of Florida, outside linebacker/edge rusher Azeez Ojulari of Georgia, cornerback Aaron Robinson of Central Florida, linebacker Elerson Smith of Northern Iowa, running back Gary Brightwell of Arizona, and cornerback Rodarius Williams of Oklahoma State.
